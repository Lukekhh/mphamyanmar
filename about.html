About MPHA Myanmar

Empowering Minds. Transforming Lives.

Our Story

MPHA Myanmar was founded with a singular vision: to bring the transformative power of hypnosis and personal development coaching to the people of Myanmar. We saw a gap in accessible, culturally-attuned mental training services—and we decided to fill it.

Over the years, our organization has grown into a trusted hub for individuals seeking clarity, confidence, and change. Through evidence-based techniques, immersive training sessions, and one-on-one coaching, we’ve helped clients overcome inner blocks and reach new heights.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through hypnosis and coaching so they can live more fulfilled, intentional, and joyful lives.

Our Vision

To become Myanmar’s leading center for transformational training, fostering a society where people are mentally strong, emotionally resilient, and consciously creating their future.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Nay Chi, our founder, is a certified clinical hypnotist with over a decade of experience in personal transformation. Her journey began with a curiosity about the subconscious mind and evolved into a mission to share the profound effects of hypnosis with others.

With her background in psychology and holistic health, she blends scientific insight with compassionate guidance. Her approach has helped countless clients break free from limiting beliefs and align with their true purpose.

Milestones & Achievements

  • 2015: MPHA Myanmar was founded in Yangon.
  • 2017: Certified over 200 students in hypnosis training.
  • 2019: Recognized as Myanmar’s leading mental coaching organization.
  • 2021: Launched community outreach programs impacting over 1,000 individuals.
  • 2023: Awarded “Best Personal Development Training” by Myanmar Wellness Association.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is hypnosis?

Hypnosis is a focused state of awareness that allows you to access your subconscious mind to facilitate positive change.

Is hypnosis safe?

Yes, hypnosis is a safe and natural process when conducted by a trained professional. It is a collaborative experience.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your goals and progress. We offer flexible plans to suit your needs.

Do you offer online training?

Yes, MPHA Myanmar provides both in-person and online coaching and training sessions for your convenience.
